    Rule notes

    What this calculator applies

    Retirement gratuity

    1/4 of last emoluments for each completed six-monthly period, capped at 16.5 times emoluments and the applicable DCRG ceiling.

    Death gratuity

    Slab based: 2x, 6x, 12x, 20x, or half-month emoluments per half-year after 20 years, capped at 33x and the DCRG ceiling.

    Service gratuity

    For retirement before minimum pensionable service: half month's emoluments for each completed six-monthly period. Verify eligibility from the service book.

    Date-based ceiling

    Auto mode uses Rs. 2.5 lakh before 2006, Rs. 7.5 lakh from 2006, Rs. 15 lakh from 2016, and Rs. 20 lakh from 01.01.2024.
