# Odisha Gratuity Calculator (OCS Pension Rules, 1992)

A simple, accurate, and transparent gratuity calculator for Odisha State Government employees based on the Odisha Civil Services (Pension) Rules, 1992.

## 🔗 Live Demo

https://11sid11.github.io/gratuity-calculator-odisha/

## 📌 Features

* Calculates retirement, death, and service gratuity
* Based on OCS Pension Rules, 1992 (Rule 49)
* Includes DA in emoluments (optional)
* Shows detailed formula breakdown for transparency
* Supports manual override of completed six-month periods
* Applies DCRG ceiling limits (₹2.5L → ₹20L based on revisions)

## 🧮 Formula Used

Gratuity = (1/4 × Last Emoluments × Completed 6-month periods)

Subject to:

* Maximum of 16.5 times emoluments
* Applicable DCRG ceiling (as per latest revisions)

## 📥 Inputs Required

* Last Basic Pay
* Dearness Allowance (%)
* Qualifying Service (Years & Months)
* Benefit Type (Retirement / Death / Service)

## ⚠️ Disclaimer

This calculator provides an estimate based on publicly available rules and government notifications. Final gratuity amounts are subject to official calculation and verification by the concerned authorities.
